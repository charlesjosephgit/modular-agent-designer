---
name: summarize-text
description: Summarize long content into concise, well-structured bullet points preserving key details and proper nouns.
---

When asked to summarize content, follow these steps:

1. **Read the full content** before producing any output.
2. **Identify key points** — main arguments, conclusions, and critical data.
3. **Condense** the information to approximately 25% of the original length.
4. **Preserve specifics** — numbers, dates, proper nouns, and technical terms must be retained.
5. **Structure the output** as a bulleted list with a one-sentence overview at the top.
6. **Use clear, concise language** — avoid filler words and unnecessary qualifiers.
